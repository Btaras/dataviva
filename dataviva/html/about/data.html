RAIS

The RAIS dataset is made up of employment data for the entirity of Brazil, down to the municipality level. It includes an average monthly wage for each classification of occupation in each industry for every location.

SECEX

The SECEX dataset is made up of export and/or production data for the entirity of Brazil, down to the municipality level. It includes an trade value (in USD) for each classification of product traded to each country for every location.

For States, this value is determined by products exported from that location. For all other levels, this value is the amount of products produced by the location. Therefore, while a product may be produced in a municipality in a state, it may be exported by a different state.
