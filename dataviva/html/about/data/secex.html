SECEX/MDIC

{% trans %}Foreign Trade Secretariat Database{% endtrans %}
{% trans %}Ministry of Development, Industry and Foreign Trade – MDIC{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}Description{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}The SECEX Database allows for the historical analysis of Brazil’s commercial exchange, one of the most important performance indicators for the economy. It describes and assesses the country’s commercial movement in relation to other world nations, encompassing purchases and sales carried out externally. It is a basic instrument in decision making and determination of economic guidelines by the Government. It allows the agents involved in the activity to improve the planning of actions through the analysis of concrete import and export data, increasing opportunities for the development of foreign trade.{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}Main Variables{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}Exports; Imports; Goods; Federation Unit; Municipalities; Destination Country; Country of Origin.{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}Data Availability{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}The database contains monthly information on Brazilian imports and exports, from 1989 onwards. The data are open to change for a period of five years. Information is gathered 15 to 20 working days after the month’s end.{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}Level of Disclosure{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}According to geographical selections: for Brazil, Major Regions, Federation Units, and Municipalities; through products according to Mercosul Common Nomenclature (NCM): Section, Chapter, Position, Sub-position, Item and Sub-item.{% endtrans %}
