{% trans %}United Nations Commodity Trade Statistics Database{% endtrans %}
{% trans %}United Nations Statistics Division{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}Description{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}The United Nations Commodity Trade Statistics Database (UN Comtrade) contains detailed imports and exports statistics reported by statistical authorities of close to 200 countries or areas. UN Comtrade is considered the most comprehensive trade database available, with more than 1 billion records. The database is continuously updated. Whenever trade data is received from the national authorities, it is standardized by the UN Statistics Division and then added to UN Comtrade.{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}Main Variables{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}Commodities; Reporters, Partners; Import; Export; Trade Value.{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}Data Availability{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}It concerns annual trade data from 1962 to the most recent year.{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}Level of Disclosure{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}Classifications of commodities: Standard International Trade Classification (SITC); Harmonized System (HS); Broad Economic Categories (BEC).{% endtrans %}
