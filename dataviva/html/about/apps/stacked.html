{% trans %}Sorting{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}Depth{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}By changing the depth shown, you can aggregate the areas to get a broad view of the Sectors, or go deeper to get a more granular look at the data.{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}Color{% endtrans %}

{% trans %}Color, along with the other visualizations, defaults to showing Sectors for the data classification you are viewing (whether it is products, industries, occupations, or locations).{% endtrans %}
