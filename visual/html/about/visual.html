Visual

What is it? A analytical decision-making tool.
Who is it for? Government, private sectors, and citizens.

Visual was a project started in August of 2012 by the Escritorio of Minas Gerais in collaboration with a small start-up called Growth Ventures based in Cambridge, MA. Visual is a tool composed of a number of applications, each of which visualize data in innovative and creative ways. Visual is intended to be used as a tool to help members of the government, private sectors, as well as citizens themselves be able to understand big data and and more importantly, use it to their advantage.



Minas Gerais

Minas Gerais is a south-eastern state of Brazil with the capital of Belo Horizonte. It is the second largest Brazilian state in population, third in Gross Domestic Product, and fourth largest by area. It is also the largest producer of coffee and milk in the country. The Escritorio of Minas Gerais is committed to constant development and innovation and is truly a leading up and coming state that is going big places.



Sabrina

Consider Sabrina your own personal guide as you explore the site. We designed Sabrina to give Data Minas a bit of life and personality. She will walk you through each of the stages of guide, prompting you with series of questions that will generate a personal report based on your specific interests. You can also ask her a question about Data Minas, Escritorio, the data we use, or any specific question you may have!
