Rings

The Rings app shows a specific data point’s primary and secondary connections in the associated Network. The connections are calculated based off of their Proximity with eachother.

RCA Scope

When looking at Product Export data, you can change the RCA calculation from using internal domestic comparisons (SECEX) to using international world trade comparisons (COMTRADE).

Color

Color, along with the other visualizations, defaults to showing Sectors for the data classification you are viewing (whether it is products, industries, occupations, or locations). In some cases, the color may be manipulated to show other values, such as growth, complexity, or opportunity gain.

Filter/Solo

By using the key on the bottom of the page, you can remove (filter) or single out (solo) specific Sectors of circles. Click the “X” on the right of the key to clear all filters and solos.

Year

The circles display data for a specific year in the dataset. By changing the year using the slider at the bottom of the app, the data associated with each circle will change to that new year’s data. This, in conjunction with using the play button to cycle through years, can provide helpful insights on how the data changes over time.
