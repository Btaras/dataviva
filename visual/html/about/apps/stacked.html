What is the Stacked app?

The Stacked app uses a visualization commonly referred to as a stacked area chart. It is a visualization used to show the change in values over time. Since the two datasets being show (exports and employment) have data ranging several years, this is a particularly useful app. At a glance we are able to see the overall trend of values as well as individual categories.

Color

The color of the different areas is used to differentiate between the different category groupings of the items being shown (this varies with the question being asked). Nesting

Another important part of any stacked visualization is its ability to show the nestedness (or hierarchy) of a dataset. Here we leverage the interactivity of the web platform to show the children of any parent category on click. This is best understood when visualizing a geographic output, for example in Where are Industries located in Brazil?, the top or parent level is the 27 Brazilian states. By clicking one of these we see the sub categories or children, which in this case are municipalities found in the selected state.
