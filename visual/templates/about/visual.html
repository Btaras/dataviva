Data Minas

What is it? A analytical decision-making tool.
Who is it for? Government, private sectors, and citizens.

Data Minas was a project started in August of 2012 by the Escritorio of Minas Gerais in collaboration with a small start-up called Growth Ventures based in Cambridge, MA. Data Minas is a tool composed of a number of applications, each of which visualize data in innovative and creative ways. Data Minas is intended to be used as a tool to help members of the government, private sectors, as well as citizens themselves be able to understand big data and and more importantly, use it to their advantage.
